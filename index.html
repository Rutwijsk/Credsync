Workflow Execution

1. Connect & Ingest

Connect ERP, invoicing, GST, logistics, and banking systems via secure connectors.

2. Normalize & Score

CredSync normalizes documents and generates risk & reliability scores for each counterparty.

3. Publish Attestations

Verified events (invoice issued, goods delivered) are published as immutable on-chain attestations.

4. Compose & Fund

Institutions compose pools via APIs. Capital providers fund pools via bank rails or stablecoins.
